**RELEASE NOTES**
Version: 1.7
Release Date: January 7, 2025

**Features:**
- **Folder Structure Preservation:** Retain source folder structures during batch conversions.
- **Enhanced Live Progress:** Display real-time counters for processed and total files.
- **Responsive UI:** App remains movable and responsive during conversion processes.
- **Improved Pop-Up Notifications:** Ensure pop-ups stay aligned at the center-top of the app window.

**Bug Fixes:**
- Resolved edge aliasing issues for RGB conversions.
- Fixed minor UI glitches in the progress bar and status labels.
- Addressed high-resolution file scaling inconsistencies.

**Performance Improvements:**
- Optimized multi-threading for faster processing of large folders.
- Reduced memory consumption for high-resolution formats.

**Known Issues:**
- **Complex AI/EPS Files:** Files with intricate layers may take longer to process.
- **Large Animated GIFs:** Processing times may increase for large or highly animated files.
