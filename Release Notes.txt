**RELEASE NOTES**  
Version: 1.8  
Release Date: August 5, 2025  

---

**✨ New Features:**  
- **HEIC Format Support:** Convert high-efficiency HEIC files (from iPhones and Apple devices) to JPG with ease.  
- **NEF (Nikon RAW) Format Support:** Directly process and convert Nikon’s NEF raw image files into standard JPGs.

---

**🔧 Enhancements (Carried Over from v1.7):**  
- **Folder Structure Preservation:** Retains the original folder structure during batch conversions.  
- **Live Progress Tracking:** Real-time display of current and total processed files.  
- **Responsive UI:** The application window remains interactive during conversions.  
- **Improved Pop-Up Placement:** Notification dialogs consistently stay top-centered within the app.

---

**🐞 Bug Fixes:**  
- Fixed edge aliasing issues in RGB image conversions.  
- Resolved minor UI misalignments in progress and status indicators.  
- Corrected scaling inconsistencies for very large image files.

---

**🚀 Performance Improvements:**  
- Enhanced multi-threading performance for batch processing.  
- Lowered RAM usage when handling high-resolution files and complex formats.

---

**⚠ Known Issues:**  
- **Complex AI/EPS Files:** Files with dense vector layers may still require longer processing times.  
- **Large Animated GIFs:** Performance may degrade with heavily animated or oversized GIFs.
